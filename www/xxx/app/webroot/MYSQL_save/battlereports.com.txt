676569 SC's armor system is easy to understand and adds depth to the game:  Fast-firing, low damage units tear apart unarmored targets (like marines vs. mutalisks) but can't do any damage to heavily fortified ones (marines vs. carriers).  Why am I explaining that on an SC forum: good question.

WarIII's armor system is entirely percentage based.  Somebody worked it out, and basically each point of armor adds around 6% "EHP" (Effective Hit Points) to a unit, so a unit with 100 HP and 3 armor can take approximately 118 points of physical damage before it dies.  The % of damage reduced deal is pretty meaningless overall, more armor = more life, don't get any more complex than that.
